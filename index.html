About This Monitor

This dashboard aggregates multiple open-source intelligence signals to estimate the probability of a US or Israeli military strike on Iran. Data is updated every 10 minutes.

Signal Sources

  • News Intel: Analyzes recent news articles for strike-related keywords and escalation indicators.
  • Civil Aviation: Monitors civilian air traffic over Iran and the Persian Gulf for unusual patterns.
  • Military Tankers: Tracks aerial refueling aircraft in the Middle East region.
  • Pentagon Pizza: Monitors activity at pizza restaurants near the Pentagon (historically correlated with late-night operations).
  • Market Odds: Prediction market probabilities from Polymarket.
  • Weather: Weather conditions over Iran (clear weather favors air operations).

This is an experimental tool for informational purposes only. Do not make decisions based on this data.

Data refreshes automatically every 10 minutes
